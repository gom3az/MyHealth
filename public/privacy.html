2. Information We Collect

MyHealth collects health and fitness data to provide you with personalized health insights and tracking capabilities. The types of data we collect include:

Health & Fitness Data

  • Step Count: Daily step tracking and activity metrics
  • Heart Rate: Heart rate measurements from wearable devices or manual entry
  • Exercise Sessions: Workout data including duration, intensity, and type of exercise
  • Wearable Device Data: Data syncs from connected wearable devices and fitness trackers
  • Sleep Patterns: Sleep duration and quality data when available
  • Weight & Body Metrics: Body weight and other body composition data you input

Device & Usage Information

We also automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device identifiers (including Android Advertising ID - ADID)
  • Crash logs and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Providing Health Services: To display your health metrics, track your fitness progress, and provide personalized insights
  2. Improving Our Services: To analyze usage patterns and enhance the functionality and user experience of MyHealth
  3. Communication: To send you important updates, notifications, and health-related information you have requested
  4. Development: To develop new features, products, and services based on aggregated health trends
